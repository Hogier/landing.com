Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most popular questions about StaffPro

How quickly can I start working with StaffPro?

Basic StaffPro implementation takes from 1 to 3 days depending on the company size. Our specialists will help you configure the system and train employees to work with the platform.

Can StaffPro be integrated with other systems?

Yes, StaffPro has an open API and ready connectors for integration with popular CRM, ERP, accounting systems and collaboration services. For specific integrations, our development team is ready to offer a custom solution.

How is data security ensured?

StaffPro uses data encryption according to the AES-256 standard, multi-factor authentication and regular backup. We comply with GDPR and the Federal Law No. 152 "On Personal Data". Our servers are located in Russia and Europe with the option to choose data storage location.

Can StaffPro be used on mobile devices?

StaffPro is available on all devices through an adaptive web interface. In addition, we have mobile apps for iOS and Android that allow you to mark entry/exit, manage tasks and receive notifications in real time.

What to do if I need help?

We provide different levels of support depending on the selected tariff — from email support to a dedicated manager. In addition, there is detailed documentation and training materials on our website.
